# What Memoir.ai Actually Is

Not a journal.
Not just search.
Not a chatbot.

It’s a **personal history operating system** that:

1. Ingests scattered digital life
2. Structures it
3. Makes it searchable
4. Analyzes patterns
5. Turns it into coherent narrative output
6. Lets you export your life in usable formats
